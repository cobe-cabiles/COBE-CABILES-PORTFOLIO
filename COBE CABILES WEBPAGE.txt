    <p class="w3-justify"> Philippines based visual artist and graphic designer who is heavily inspired by the lowbrow pop-surrealism movement in which the oddities of things transpire. His creative works also have a sense of experimentalism and modern culture touches. Merging the beautifications of ages with our contemporary world in such surrealistic expression. </p>
    
    <p class=MsoNormal align=center style='text-align:center'><span lang=EN

      <h2 class="w3-wide w3-center">MULTIMEDIA ARTS</h2>
      <p class="w3-opacity w3-center"><i>"The Instruments that Breathes Art"</i></p><br>

     <p class="w3-justify"> Multimedia Arts, what really is Multimedia Arts? According to www.study.com , “Multimedia Artists focus on the innovation and technology associated with the study of new media. Where students are trained to design and create new media projects spanning print, web, animation and film. They also acquire a foundation in the visual arts, critical theory and computer science. Aspiring professionals also investigate the convergence of art and technology, as well as the emergence of new technologies in the field.”</p>
     
       <p class=MsoNormal align=center style='text-align:center'><span lang=EN
style='font-family:"Montserrat SemiBold";color:#F3F3F3'>&nbsp;</span></p>
     
     <p class="w3-justify"> Generally, we define art as a form of communication that allows people from different cultures and different times to communicate ideologies with each other through visuals, sounds and stories. Art paves way for social change and can give voice when it comes to political, social and personal matters. Any form of art can give an emotion that can make an individual enlightenment to a certain act.  (Paint and Party, 2020)</p>
     
       <p class=MsoNormal align=center style='text-align:center'><span lang=EN
style='font-family:"Montserrat SemiBold";color:#F3F3F3'>&nbsp;</span></p>
     
     <p class="w3-justify"> Referring to the terminology, “Multimedia”, with its various forms such as: text, graphics, sound, animation, pictures, and videos. In our modern days, it is an important key for people to connect. As our world continues to sophisticate and develop, technology is a gift for faster works and easier ways to process and produce things. As it is connected to “Multimedia Arts”, reaching out artistry through technology will ease the distribution of messages to society. Multimedia arts, with its various forms, can help the society develop web designs and the entertainment industry in the goal of perceiving messages for the society to learn. (C.I.I.T. College of Arts and Technology,  2017 )Thus came the part where Multimedia Artists would do Animations and visual interpretations according to the feedback from Art directors,  Fellow Animators, Game Designers/Developers, or clients. Of course, this project would allow conversations and connections with the clients, other animators, games designers, directors, and other staff (which may include actors) to review deadlines and development timelines.</p>
     
     <p class=MsoNormal align=center style='text-align:center'><span lang=EN
style='font-family:"Montserrat SemiBold";color:#F3F3F3'>&nbsp;</span></p>

     <p class="w3-justify w3-opacity"> Below are  some of my creative works from both 2nd year subjects and 3rd year subjects that are aligned to my major course which is Multimedia Arts. Most of this creative work allowed me to experiment on creative styles and it is honestly such genuine joy to see the progression of my way of creatively approaching these output designs. </p>
